About

Miraat is a mental health platform dedicated to helping individuals understand, manage, and improve their emotional well-being. With personalized assessments, AI-powered chatbot support, and valuable resources, Miraat provides a safe space for users to track their progress, find expert guidance, and access mental health tools to enhance their overall well-being. Your journey to a healthier mind starts here.
